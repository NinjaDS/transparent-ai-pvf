❌ Black-Box AI (Current Standard)

• Model produces a score — no explanation of why

• SHAP / LIME values are approximations, not the actual reasoning

• Catastrophic forgetting: retraining on new data destroys prior knowledge

• EU AI Act 2026 (Article 13): high-risk decisions require human-readable justification — black-box models cannot comply by design

• JPMorgan, Barclays: credit decisions challenged in court when model can't explain itself

"Their design makes it impossible to fully control how they learn and store information." — Prof. Alexander Balanov, Loughborough University (2026)

✅ Plastic Vector Fields (PVF)

• Every learning step, memory update and inference is mathematically traceable

• Transparency is a structural property — not a dashboard added afterwards

• Continuous learning without forgetting: new signals reinforce or release old patterns, never overwrite

• Every decision produces a full reasoning trace — audit-ready for regulators

• Published: Janson & Balanov, Physica D: Nonlinear Phenomena, 30 April 2026
